     1.Decision Making Statement:
        1.if statement
        2.if-else statement
     2.Looping Statement:
        1.for condition
        2.while condition
     3.Jumping Statement:
        1.continue
        2.break
     
3.What is the Difference betweeen break and continue statements?
1.Break Statements:
     1.The break statement is used to terminate the loop immediately.
     2.We can use a break with the switch statement.
     3.The break statement terminates the whole loop early.
2.Continue Statements:
     1.The continue statement is used to skip the current iteration of the loop.
     2.We can not use a continue with the switch statement.
     3.The break statement terminates the whole loop early.

5.When will you get ArrayIndexOutOfBoundsException?
      ArrayIndexOutOfBoundsException occurs when we access an array, or a Collection, 
that is backed by an array with an invalid index.
